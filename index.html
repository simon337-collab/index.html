Key guidelines for 55+
🦴
Joint-friendly form
Prioritize controlled reps over heavy weight. Never sacrifice form to add load. Machines and cables are great alternatives to free weights early on.
😴
Recovery is training
At 55, muscle repair takes 48–72 hrs. Never train the same muscle group on back-to-back days. Sleep 7–9 hrs — growth hormone peaks during deep sleep.
❤️
Zone 2 cardio
Zone 2 (60–70% max HR) is the fat-burning sweet spot. Target HR: ~99–116 bpm. Talk test: you can speak in sentences but not sing.
🧘
Flexibility daily
Even 10 min of daily stretching reduces injury risk dramatically. Focus on hips, hamstrings, thoracic spine, and shoulders.
📈
Progressive overload
Increase load by 5–10% only when you can complete all sets with clean form. Log weights and reps weekly.
💊
Supplement basics
Consider creatine (5g/day), vitamin D3, magnesium glycinate, and omega-3s. Discuss with your doctor first.

For general fitness purposes only. Consult a physician before beginning any new exercise program, especially if you have pre-existing conditions, joint issues, or cardiovascular concerns.
